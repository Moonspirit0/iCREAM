About Us

Established in 1967, our goal since then has always been to serve our customers the utmost best.

Since she was a little, our owner, Kuhoo Srivastava, has always had a love for ice cream. Growing up, there was nothing more she looked forward to as much as making homemade ice cream on a hot summer day. Perhaps this is what motivated her to start iCREAM, your local ice cream store today. Our goal, and hers too, is to serve our customers in a way that they'll never miss their own homemade ice cream making memories.
